How do I sell cryptocurrency?

Enter your details and send the exact amount of cryptocurrency to the unique wallet address shown in the next sell page. If we do not receive the correct amount within the 15 minutes, we will have to recalculate the value of the sent amount to the current sell rate of the coin.

When will I receive my payout?

Payouts to bank accounts are processed twice a day. Please keep in mind that it can take up to three business days before the payout arrives in your bank account.

Good to know:
  • There is a different limit for a sell order for every cryptocurrency. This way we can guarantee the the agreed sell price of the order. If you would like to sell even more than the current sell limit, you can always place multiple sell orders.
  • Please make sure that you add enough miners fees to the cryptocurrency transaction so we can process the sell order in time.
  • Unfortunately, our bank does not process payouts during holidays and weekend. The payouts will be processed the next business day after the weekend/holiday.
