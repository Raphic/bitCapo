Why a BitCapo account?

By having a BitCapo account you will have access to the world of cryptocurrencies without needing to go through the learning process of what they are and how to use them.

The BitCapo account is connected to multiple crypto networks which allows it to store your cryptocurrency for you, this is also made possible through the regulatory crypto custodial license that it has successfully obtained.

What does this mean?

This means that BitCapo is able to provide a 24/7 secure storage feature for you, for free.

On top of that you are able to make use of our instant messaging customer support which is able to provide you with any possible assistance. The customer support is also operational 24/7.
